Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel a subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your platform account. On iOS, open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions. On Android, open Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.

How do I request a refund?
Refunds for in-app purchases are handled by the platform store. On iOS, visit reportaproblem.apple.com. On Android, visit Google Play's "Order History" and tap "Report a problem".

How do I delete my account or data?
Email akashmangukiya10@gmail.com with the subject "Account deletion request" and we will process it within 30 days.
